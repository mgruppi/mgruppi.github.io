About me

I am a PhD student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (since 2017). My research interests include machine learning and natural language processing methods and applications, particularly to misinformation, and language change and bias.

Currently, my research focus on automatic methods for the detection of language change. My goal is to find ways to understand and explain the divergence in language usage in different domains, and how those can be used by humans and machines.
